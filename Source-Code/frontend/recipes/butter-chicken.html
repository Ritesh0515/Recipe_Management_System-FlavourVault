Butter Chicken

Rich and creamy tomato-based chicken curry with aromatic Indian spices, perfect with naan or rice.

Prep Time: 15 minutes

Cook Time: 40 minutes

Servings: 4

Ingredients

  • 500g chicken thighs, cubed
  • 1 cup yogurt
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 1 cup tomato puree
  • 2 tsp garam masala
  • 1 tsp turmeric
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • Salt to taste

Instructions

  1. Marinate chicken with yogurt and spices for at least 1 hour.
  2. Melt butter in a pan and sauté garlic.
  3. Add chicken and cook until lightly browned.
  4. Add tomato puree and simmer for 20 minutes.
  5. Stir in garam masala and turmeric, cook for 5 more minutes.
  6. Serve hot with naan or rice.
