Grilled Chicken

Tender chicken marinated with aromatic spices and grilled to perfection.

Prep Time: 20 minutes

Cook Time: 25 minutes

Servings: 4

Ingredients

  • 4 chicken breasts
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tsp paprika
  • 1 tsp garlic powder
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice

Instructions

  1. Mix olive oil, paprika, garlic powder, salt, pepper, and lemon juice to create marinade.
  2. Coat chicken breasts with marinade and refrigerate for 30 minutes.
  3. Preheat grill to medium-high heat.
  4. Grill chicken for 6-7 minutes per side, until cooked through and juices run clear.
  5. Rest chicken for 5 minutes before serving.
