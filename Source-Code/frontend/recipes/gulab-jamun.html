Gulab Jamun

Soft, deep-fried milk dumplings soaked in rose-flavored sugar syrup. A beloved Indian dessert.

Prep Time: 20 minutes

Cook Time: 25 minutes

Servings: 6

Ingredients

  • 1 cup milk powder
  • 1/4 cup all-purpose flour
  • 2 tbsp ghee
  • 1/2 cup milk (to knead dough)
  • 2 cups sugar
  • 2 cups water
  • Few drops rose water

Instructions

  1. Mix milk powder, flour, and ghee in a bowl.
  2. Add milk slowly and knead into soft dough.
  3. Shape small balls from dough.
  4. Heat oil and deep-fry balls until golden brown.
  5. Prepare sugar syrup with sugar, water, and rose water.
  6. Soak fried balls in syrup for at least 1 hour.
