Fresh Juice

Natural fruit juice made from seasonal fruits. Refreshing, vitamin-rich, and naturally sweet.

Prep Time: 10 minutes

Cook Time: 0 minutes

Servings: 2

Ingredients

  • 3 oranges, peeled
  • 2 apples, cored
  • 1 carrot, chopped
  • 1-inch ginger piece
  • Ice cubes (optional)

Instructions

  1. Combine all ingredients in a juicer or blender.
  2. Blend until smooth.
  3. Pour into glasses and add ice if desired.
  4. Serve immediately.
