Fresh Salad

Crisp mixed vegetables with a tangy dressing. Light, refreshing, and packed with nutrients.

Prep Time: 15 minutes

Cook Time: 0 minutes

Servings: 2

Ingredients

  • 2 cups mixed lettuce
  • 1 cucumber, sliced
  • 1 tomato, diced
  • 1/4 cup sliced red onions
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Combine all vegetables in a large bowl.
  2. Whisk olive oil, lemon juice, salt, and pepper to make dressing.
  3. Pour dressing over vegetables and toss gently.
  4. Serve immediately for freshness.
